Plan Your Trip

Best Time to Visit

Oct–Feb: Pleasant. Mar–May: Warm. Jun–Sep: Monsoon with lush gardens.

Transport

  • 🚇 Namma Metro connects key corridors.
  • 🚌 BMTC buses are frequent on main routes.
  • 🚕 App cabs and autos (use the meter when possible).

Where to Stay

Budget: MG Road, Majestic. Mid-range: Indiranagar, Jayanagar. Luxury: CBD, UB City vicinity, Koramangala.

Day Trips

  • Nandi Hills (~60km)
  • Ramanagara (~50km)
  • Savandurga (~50km)
  • Mysuru (~150km)

Practical Info

  • 📶 Local SIMs available widely; strong 4G/5G coverage.
  • 💳 UPI and cards widely accepted; carry small cash.
  • 🙏 Temples: modest dress, remove shoes.
  • 🗣️ Kannada basics help; English/Hindi commonly understood in tourist areas.
