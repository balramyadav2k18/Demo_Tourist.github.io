Quick Highlights

Gardens

Lalbagh and Cubbon Park—morning walks, glasshouse shows, and tree-lined avenues.

Heritage

Bangalore Palace, Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace, and Vidhana Soudha exteriors.

Nature

Bannerghatta safaris, Nandi Hills sunrise trips, and lakeside sunsets.

Food & Cafes

Filter coffee, crisp dosas, donne biryani, and third-wave cafes.

Nightlife

Microbreweries, live gigs, and late-night bites in Indiranagar & Koramangala.

Shopping

Commercial Street bargains to UB City luxury—mix it up in one day.
