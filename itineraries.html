Bengaluru Itineraries

1-Day Plan

  • Morning: Lalbagh walk, filter coffee nearby.
  • Afternoon: Bangalore Palace tour; lunch in MG Road area.
  • Evening: UB City/Church Street stroll, microbrewery dinner.

Walk or use Metro between central stops; cabs for longer hops.

2-Day Plan

  • Day 1: Lalbagh → Tipu’s Palace → VV Puram food street evening.
  • Day 2: Cubbon Park → Vidhana Soudha (exterior) → HAL Museum.

Combine Metro/Bus (BMTC) with cabs. Start early for parks.

3-Day Plan

  • Day 1: Central heritage and parks.
  • Day 2: Bannerghatta Biological Park (half-day) + cafes.
  • Day 3: Nandi Hills sunrise day trip; return for shopping.

Check opening hours and weather; monsoon adds lush greenery.
